Why this is different

Most insurance conversations start with price.

CoverageFit starts with whether the coverage actually fits. Price matters — but only after the policy is structured around the real risk.

The goal is simple: Catch the questions that usually show up during claims, lender deadlines, rental issues, or family emergencies — before they become expensive surprises.
