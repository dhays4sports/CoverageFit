Most people confuse “having insurance” with being protected.

Home insurance protects the structure. Life insurance, income protection, and related coverage can help protect the people paying for the home — not just the structure itself.

Assumption: “The house is insured, so we’re good.”
Reality: The bigger question is what happens if income disappears.
