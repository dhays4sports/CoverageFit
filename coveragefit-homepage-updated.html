Why this is different

Most insurance conversations start with price.

CoverageFit starts with whether the coverage actually fits. Price matters — but only after the policy is structured around the real risk.

The goal is simple: Catch the questions that usually show up during claims, lender deadlines, rental issues, or family emergencies — before they become expensive surprises.

How CoverageFit works

Each tool is designed to connect: quick score, personalized insight, then a practical review path with Dylan.

1 Run a quick score Answer a few plain-English questions to surface areas that may deserve attention.
2 See your report Get a personalized summary of what appears stronger and what may need review.
3 Review the actual policy See what deserves review, quoting, or adjustment based on the real policy details.
