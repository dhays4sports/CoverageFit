What I’ll look for

This review is designed to catch the areas that can create surprises after a commercial apartment claim — before the building has to rely on the coverage.

Rental income / loss of rentsWhether the amount and timeframe could help cash flow during repairs.
Liability and umbrella structureWhether limits fit tenants, guests, contractors, common areas, stairs, walkways, and parking.
Ordinance and replacement costWhether code upgrades or building valuation could create financial pressure.
Water damage and deductiblesWhether plumbing, water-loss limitations, deductibles, and prevention deserve attention.
