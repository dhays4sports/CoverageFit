The mistake is assuming the policy is built right.

Landlord claims can expose things a standard quote conversation never surfaced.

Policy type mismatch

A rental property should be structured for non-owner-occupied use, not treated like a primary home.

Income interruption

If the unit becomes unlivable after a covered loss, rental income may stop while repairs are underway.

Liability exposure

Tenants, guests, vendors, and visitors create more moving parts than a normal home policy.

Most landlords do not find these gaps until a claim forces the issue.

This page is designed to compress the decision: confirm what matters, send the right details, and get a clearer policy review path.
